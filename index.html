Introduction

TFAVision presents an innovative solution that addresses a critical need in urban planning and infrastructure development. We are providing the traffic engineers with a continuous data stream for non-motorist (bikes & pedestrians) traffic flow. This helps in taking major infrastructure decisions based on sound data-driven analysis.
